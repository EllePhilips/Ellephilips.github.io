About Us

At Genesis Salon, our mission is to create a space for people with all different types of hair. We want to be a part in generating more accessibility in hair salons for people with textured hair. We are committed to continuously learning new techniques for treating all hair types. Additionally we hope to offer a space with music, laughter and singing providing a celebratory event for a change of style.
